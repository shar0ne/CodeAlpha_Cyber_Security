Phishing Awareness Module

💡 What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack technique where criminals masquerade as trustworthy entities to trick you into revealing sensitive data like passwords or banking details. They rely heavily on Social Engineering—manipulating human psychology through fear, reward, or urgency.

Key Indicators to Watch For:

  • 🚨 Spoofed Domains: Look closely at sender addresses; attackers mimic official brands using free or slightly misspelled domains.
  • ⏳ Artificial Urgency: Demands for immediate payment or warnings about compromised tokens are designed to make you act without thinking.
  • 💸 Advance Fee Scams: Legitimate financial networks won't ask for a deposit to clear pending assets.
